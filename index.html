The Event

Looking to inspire and challenge our attendees, HackGT’s 5th annual 36-hour hackathon will help students develop their skills, explore new technologies, and turn their curiosities into opportunities. Participants are invited to discover friendly campgrounds, lush landscapes, and a spirit of adventure — inspired by the diverse beauty of America's National Parks from Yellowstone and the Grand Canyon to the Olympic Peninsula and Denali.

FAQ

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, headphones, and anything else you need. We'll supply hardware, meals, and snacks.

Where is HackGT 5?

The event will be taking place on Georgia Tech campus in the Klaus Advanced Computing Building.

Who is eligible?

Any student over the age of 18 enrolled in a college or university is allowed to participate, but anyone can be a mentor or volunteer. Teams can be up to four members - you can register with your team when you apply, or any time after you’re accepted, but you can also create a team at the event!

If you are younger than 18, but a Georgia Tech student, you can still participate.

Are there any costs?

The event is free, thanks to our sponsors! We will offer travel reimbursement -- just indicate where you’re coming from when you apply, and we’ll reach out to you.

Can I use past projects?

True to the spirit of hackathons, you may not submit your past projects to HackGT.

Do I need any experience to apply?

No! Experience is not required. Hackathons are open environments for everyone. If you’re a GT student, make sure to like our Facebook page for updates on HackGTeeny, our workshop-filled day of hackathon prep exclusive for Georgia Tech students.

Check out our submissions from last year, including a multi-language transcompiler, a hackathon team finder, and a video frame finder! Download and try them out yourself! Don’t be afraid to think big!

What are Campsites?

Think office hours - regroup, refocus, re-engage! Each path will have the opportunity to come together, meet face to face, and receive specialized help from our mentors and sponsors throughout the event.

More questions? Ask away! Our adventure experts are happy to help so send us an email at hello@hack.gt!
